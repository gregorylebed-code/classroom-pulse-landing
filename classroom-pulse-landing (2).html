01

Documentation Without the Drudgery

Stop wasting your prep period on data entry. This app handles the organization while you focus on the students.

🎙️ Smart Note-Taking
Record observations via voice or text. The AI handles tagging and categorization automatically — no manual labels needed.
⌨️ Custom Shorthand
Use your own abbreviations like "ss" for Social Studies. The app expands them for you and even suggests new ones based on your habits.
🏷️ Tailored Indicators
Use pre-installed tags for behavior and communication, or create your own to match your specific classroom needs.

05

SEL Lesson Plans, Written by Your Data

The app reads your class notes and surfaces three targeted SEL lesson ideas — matched to what your students actually need this week. Pick one and get a complete, ready-to-run lesson in seconds.

🧠 Data-Driven Suggestions
The AI analyzes recent notes and recommends themes like Empathy, Belonging, Conflict, Focus, Kindness, or Respect — based on what it's actually seeing in your classroom.
📖 Full Lesson, One Tap
Each lesson includes a warm-up, main activity, and exit ticket — completely generated and ready to deliver. You can also pick your own curriculum and the AI will adapt.
🔁 No Repeats, Ever
Pulse tracks every lesson delivered to each class so it never suggests one you've already done. Your history is always one tap away.

06

Track Your Impact & Level Up

Every note you log and every report you generate earns you time back. Pulse tallies it all and shows you exactly how many hours of admin work you've reclaimed — plus milestone badges as you level up your usage.

⏱️ Time Saved Counter
3 minutes per note. 15 minutes per report. Watch your total reclaimed time grow as the year goes on — it adds up fast.
🏆 Milestone Badges
Hit thresholds for notes logged, reports generated, and hours saved. Badges light up as you earn them — from "Just Getting Started 🌱" all the way to "Pulse Legend 🏆".
📊 At-a-Glance Stats
Your personal impact dashboard lives in Settings — always updated, always honest about how much lighter your workload has gotten.

A Message from the Creator

I have spent over 20 years in the classroom. Before I became a 3rd grade teacher, I worked as a one-to-one aide and a Registered Behavior Technician. I know exactly how loud, fast, and overwhelming a school day can be. I also know that high-quality, data-backed insights are the only way to truly understand what a student needs.

I didn't build this app to give you more work. I built it because I was tired of drowning in paperwork while trying to keep my head above water. This tool was created with love to assist my fellow teachers who are looking for a better way to track progress and bridge the gap between school and home.

"Good data shouldn't be a chore. It should be the bridge that connects a teacher's observation to a parent's understanding."
