01
Logging

Log a Note in Under 10 Seconds

Tap a student's name, type (or dictate) a quick observation, and hit save. No menu diving, no forms — just a clean input and you're back to teaching. The AI auto-tags the note and fills in indicators like "behavior" or "academic" so you never have to categorize manually.

🎙
Voice input — dictate notes hands-free while moving around the room.
⌨️
Custom shorthand — type "ss gw" and it expands to "Social Studies — great work".
🏷️
Auto-tagging — behavior, mood, and communication flags applied instantly.
🎬

Student Profiles

Every Student's Story at a Glance

Tap any student to open their full history — every note, tag, and communication log, all in one scrollable timeline. Spot patterns instantly with color-coded indicators and trend badges. No spreadsheet hunting required.

📋
Full history timeline — every observation logged, sorted by date.
📈
Indicator trends — see if behavior is improving or slipping week over week.
📁
Contact info attached — parent phone, email, and communication log all in one place.

03
AI Summaries

One Tap. Full Class Summary.

Hit the Summarize button and the AI reads every note logged this week — or this month, or this year — and returns a concise narrative broken down by student. Ask follow-up questions like "Who had the roughest Wednesday?" and get specific answers.

🤖
One-tap summaries — daily, weekly, or custom date range on demand.
💬
Ask anything — interactive AI assistant answers questions about your class data.
✏️
Edit before you send — tweak AI-generated drafts until the wording is exactly right.
🎬

04
Parent Communication

Parent Emails in One Tap

From any student's profile, tap "Contact Parent" and the app opens a pre-filled Gmail or text with the recipient, subject line, and a data-backed message body already written. All you do is review and send.

📨
Pre-filled messages — AI writes the email body based on recent notes.
📇
Smart contact sync — upload a CSV and AI maps parents to the right students automatically.
📝
Communication log — every outreach is timestamped and attached to the student's record.
🎬

05
Schedule & Calendar

What's Next — Always Pinned to the Top

Scan your school calendar once. Pulse pins your next upcoming event to the top of the screen and shows a six-day specials rotation lookahead — so you always know if it's a gym day before the kids ask.

📅
Calendar banner — next event always visible, skips to tomorrow after 4 PM.
🔄
Specials rotation — six-day lookahead for art, gym, music, library, and more.
📤
Easy import — paste your calendar from a spreadsheet or scan it with AI.
🎬

Reports & Export

Reports Ready for Any Meeting

Generate a formatted PDF progress report for any student — covering any timeframe — with one tap. Pull it up at a parent conference, paste it into an IEP, or email it directly. No reformatting, no copy-paste, no mess.

📄
PDF export — formatted, professional reports generated instantly.
📆
Any date range — one day, one month, one school year — your choice.
💾
Bulk export — download reports for your entire class at once.

07
SEL Lesson Plans

AI Reads Your Notes.
Writes the Lesson.

After summarizing your class, Pulse offers to suggest SEL lesson topics based on what it actually saw in your notes this week. Pick one of three AI-generated options — or enter your own curriculum — and get a complete, ready-to-deliver lesson plan in seconds.

🧠
3 themed suggestions — Empathy, Belonging, Focus, Conflict, Kindness, or Respect, matched to your class data.
📖
Full lesson generated — opener, main activity, and exit ticket, ready to run immediately.
🔁
No repeats — Pulse tracks every lesson delivered per class and never suggests one you've already done.
🎬

08
Your Impact

See How Much Time
You've Taken Back

Every note and report you generate adds to a running tally of time saved — 3 minutes per note, 15 minutes per report. Pulse turns that into an hours counter and lights up milestone badges as you level up through the year.

⏱️
Time saved counter — real-time estimate of admin hours reclaimed, calculated automatically.
🏆
Milestone badges — notes, reports, and hours each have their own badge track that lights up as you hit them.
📈
Levels up over time — from "Just Getting Started 🌱" to "Super Note-Taker ⭐" to "Pulse Legend 🏆".
🎬
